Quiver is a tool to plot direction fields of first-order differential equations. It is intended to be a quick and simple tool, and supports the following mathematical operations:

  • Addition and subtraction
  • Multiplication, division, and exponentiation
  • Sine and cosine
  • Natural exponentiation and natural logarithm

Quiver is written in Python, and uses SymPy and matplotlib to generate and plot equations. Variables are assumed to be named "x" and "y" and mathematical operators should be entered with Python syntax (+, -, *, /, **, sin, cos, exp and log).
